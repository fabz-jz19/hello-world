Le Fangourin Restaurant

What They Do

Amidst the garden of Beau plan, at the heart of the L'Aventure du Sucre's, the Fangourin restaurant presents an exceptional setting: century-old vegetation, a veranda overlooking the central plateau’s mountains.With its perfectly preserved vegetation, its pictures with lush greenery and geese, the restaurant offers a stress free environment conducive to relaxation.For all of your celebrations, the Fangourin restaurant suggests a wide range of lunches and dinners, with the chefs recommended set menus, buffets, or cocktails.The Fangourin cuisine is inspired from unique flavors while maintaining a rich Mauritian culinary heritage. With a cuisine both refined and festive, that infuses new flavors while preserving the Mauritian culinary tradition, the Fangourin offers services ranging from cocktails and hors d'oeuvres to full dinners.
